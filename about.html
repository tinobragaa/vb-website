About   Me

I'm a software engineer and MSc Computer Science student at UCD, specializing in dynamic, responsive, and interactive digital experiences.

In 2021, I graduated with a First Class Bachelor's degree in Law from Estácio de Sá University in Brazil. After completing my degree, I decided to move to Ireland in search of new opportunities and challenges.

Once in Ireland, I made the decision to shift my career focus. I enrolled in a Bootcamp with Code Institute and graduated in 2023, acquiring skills in web development and coding.

With over 6 years of experience in customer service, I am now fully committed to my new path and currently pursuing my MSc in Computer Science at University College Dublin (UCD).
